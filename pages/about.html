My name is Andrew Gardner, and I am the author of this website. I am a Pinelands native (or a "Piney" as some would say), I grew up in Indian Mills and I have a deep love and appreciation for the area. I've had such an amazing life exploring the Pinelands and discovering new things to do, new places to see, and new history to learn that I wanted to give that same opportunity to as many people as possible. If this website at all intrigued you, I encourage you to visit the Pinelands not only to see its beauty for yourself, but also to support the communities and the wide variety of small businesses in the area.
